1⁄3 cup sugar
1⁄2 cup mayonnaise
1⁄4 cup milk
1⁄2 cup buttermilk
2 1⁄2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1⁄2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoon rice vinegar
1⁄2 teaspoon salt
1⁄8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp of marjoram
3 cloves of garlic, finely minced

1/2 medium onion, finely shredded
1/2 green bell pepper, finely shredded
2 (10 ounce) packagesfinely shredded cabbage (angel hair)
1 carrot, shredded

Whisk together first 8 ingredients in a large bowl until blended. Add cabbage and carrot to bowl, and toss to coat. Cover and chill at least 2 hours.
