2 small fennel bulbs (1.5 lb total), trimmed of tough outside leaves or ribs (leaving 1lb total)

3 tablespoons good olive oil
2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
2 tbp fresh lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon tabasco sauce
2 tsp fennel seeds


shave the fennelb ulbs into thin slices on a japanese slicer or mandoline (~5cups). combine the olive oil, lemon zest and juice, salt, tabasco, and fennel seeds into a medium bowl. add the fennel, and toss to coat it with the dressing. Arrange some fennel on each plate and top with a few slices of the salmon rolls. If desired, drizzle with olive oil.
